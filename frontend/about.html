About Global News Network

For over 25 years, we've been delivering trusted, accurate, and timely news to audiences worldwide. Our commitment to journalistic integrity and excellence has made us a leading source of global news.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Global News Network began as a small newspaper with a big vision: to provide unbiased, comprehensive news coverage to readers around the world. What started as a humble publication has grown into a multimedia news organization with correspondents in over 150 countries.

Our journey has been guided by a simple but powerful principle: the public's right to know. We believe that access to accurate information is fundamental to a functioning democracy and an informed society.

Today, we reach millions of readers, viewers, and listeners across digital, print, and broadcast platforms, maintaining our founding commitment to journalistic excellence and integrity.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to deliver accurate, timely, and impactful journalism that informs the public, holds power accountable, and contributes to a better-informed world. We believe in the power of truth to transform societies and improve lives.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of journalistic integrity, ensuring accuracy, fairness, and transparency in all our reporting.

Transparency

We are open about our sources, methods, and corrections. We admit mistakes and correct them promptly and prominently.

Inclusivity

We strive to represent diverse voices and perspectives in our reporting, ensuring all communities are heard and represented.

Courage

We have the courage to pursue difficult stories, challenge power, and report truth even when it's unpopular or dangerous.

Global Perspective

We recognize that today's most pressing issues transcend borders and require global understanding and context.

Public Service

We believe journalism is a public service, essential for democracy and the betterment of society.

Our History

1995

Foundation

Global News Network is founded by a group of journalists committed to independent, fact-based reporting. Our first newspaper is published with a circulation of 50,000.

2001

First International Bureau

We open our first international bureau in London, marking the beginning of our global expansion and commitment to international coverage.

2008

Digital Transformation

We launch our comprehensive digital platform, becoming one of the first major news organizations to fully embrace online journalism.

2015

20th Anniversary & Global Reach

Celebrating 20 years of journalism excellence, we now have bureaus in 50 countries and reach over 20 million readers monthly.

2020

Pandemic Coverage Excellence

Our comprehensive COVID-19 coverage is recognized with multiple awards, including the Pulitzer Prize for Public Service.

2023

AI Journalism Initiative

We launch our AI-assisted journalism initiative, using technology to enhance reporting while maintaining human editorial oversight.
